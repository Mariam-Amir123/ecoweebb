Learn About Plastics

What are Plastics?

Plastics are synthetic materials that pose a significant environmental threat. They can come from a variety of sources including the breakdown of larger plastic items and microbeads in personal care products.

Solutions

To combat plastic pollution, consider reducing plastic use, supporting policies that limit plastic production, and participating in clean-up initiatives.
